Salaam!

Influenced by the Arab spring, globalisation, and social media, Arabic music is now as diverse as ever. Therefore the music that Salaam brings is united in its Arabic identity, but differs in genre and color. Salaam aims to introduce you to the surge of new, modern and reinterpreted sounds of Arabic music ranging from trap, rap, RnB, pop and electronic music. Throughout this blog we will be discussing Arabic art communities, collectives, artists and musicians and promoting festivals and events with Arabic identity.
